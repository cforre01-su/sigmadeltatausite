The annual philanthropy events that the chapter of Sigma Delta Tau at Syracuse University hold are “Eat Pie” and “Besdt in the Bakery.” The money raised in these events support the non-profit organizations: Jewish Women International (JWI) and Prevent Child Abuse America (PCAA). JWI is the leading Jewish organization that works to empower women and girls by ensuring and protecting their physical and economic security, promoting and celebrating inter-generational leadership and inspiring civic participation and community engagement. PCAA promotes services to help improve the well-being of children in all 50 states and develops programs to prevent all types of abuse and neglect. Both of these organization, JWI and PCAA, embody our chapter’s values: empowering women, sisterhood, scholarship, leadership and service. Our chapter’s values stem back to the day we were founded on March 25, 1917, when seven young Jewish women who exemplified self-confidence and the willingness to take a chance founded us, Sigma Delta Tau, at Cornell University.
